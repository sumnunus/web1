D.P.: Season 2

An Jun-Ho (Jung Hae-In) and Han Ho-Yeol (Koo Gyo-Hwan) attempt to recover from the traumatic events surrounding the capture of Private Cho Suk-Bong (Cho Hyun-Chul) in their own ways.

They continue their service in the D.P. team that attempts to capture military deserters.

Meanwhile, Sergeant Park Beom-Gu (Kim Sung-Kyun) feels pressure from higher ups to cover up recent events. A mass shooting takes place in a military barrack after a soldier was mercilessly bullied.
